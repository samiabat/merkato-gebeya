About Merkato Gebeya

Our Story of
Growth & Community

Launched in 2024, Merkato Gebeya is Ethiopia's premier online shopping marketplace with an active presence across the country. Our name — "Merkato Gebeya" (ሜርካቶ ገበያ) — embodies the spirit of Ethiopia's iconic market district, now brought to your fingertips.

We support a wide range of tailored marketing, data, and service solutions. With 10,500+ sellers, 300 brands, and over 3 million customers served, we offer a diverse assortment in categories ranging from consumer electronics, fashion, home appliances, and much more.
